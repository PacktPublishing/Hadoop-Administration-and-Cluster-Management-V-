$ hdfs balancer

HDFS copy operations

$ hadoop fs –put <file> /
$ hadoop fs –copyFromLocal <file> /
$ hdfs dfs –put <file> /

$ hadoop fs –get /<file> .
$ hadoop fs –copyToLocal /<file> .
$ hdfs dfs –get /<file> .

$ hadoop fs –cp /<file> /path
$ hadoop fs –mv /<file> /path

Distcp

Runs MapReduce to do the copy operation.

$ hadoop distcp /source /dest
